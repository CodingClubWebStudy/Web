about us

Welcoming bakery and cafe offering coffee, croissant-centric menu, plus wine & beer later in the day. We take a great deal of pride in the food we serve. From our coffees and teas to our meats and cheeses, all of our ingredients are of the highest quality. Whenever possible we use local, organic and fair-trade purveyors. We bake all of our breads and pastries throughout the day in order to ensure that our customers will always experience the best of what we have to offer. We accommodate our customers in every way possible; if you have any special requests, please do not hesitate to let us know.
